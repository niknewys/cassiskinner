Feast — a first body of work

The warmth of
a shared meal

View the gallery

Paintings of food, family, and the act of sharing a table.

Feast draws from memories of cooking, gathering and dining together — capturing the movement, sounds and generosity that unfold around a busy kitchen and dining table.

Through rich colour and loose, moving strokes, the works hold the emotional atmosphere of shared meals: hands reaching, conversations overlapping, empty plates and full hearts.

About the artist

Cassi Skinner is a Sunshine Coast–based artist and art educator whose work explores food, dining, and the connections formed around shared meals.

Working predominantly in oil paint and gouache, Cassi captures the atmosphere and emotion of everyday moments in the kitchen and around the dining table. Her practice moves between realism and looser, more expressive brushwork, allowing each piece to convey both the intimacy and energy of these familiar spaces.

Cassi holds a Bachelor degree in Design & Marketing and has taught at Art School Co for the past eight years. Feast is her first body of work.
